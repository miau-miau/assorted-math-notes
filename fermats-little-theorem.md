#### Fermat's Little Theorem
$$
\textnormal{If }p\textnormal{ is prime and }p\nmid c,\textnormal{ then }c^{p-1}\equiv 1\;(\textnormal{mod }p)
$$
(see [[congruence]])

>[!example]
>$512^{12}\;(\textnormal{mod }7)\equiv(513^6)^2\equiv 1^2\equiv 1\;(\textnormal{mod }7)$
