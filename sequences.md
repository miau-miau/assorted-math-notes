>[!definition]
>A **sequence** is an infinite list of real numbers $a_{1},a_{2},a_{3}\dots$.
>Or, alternatively, a [[functions|function]] $f:\mathbb{N}\to \mathbb{R}\quad f(n)=a_{n}$.
>
>>[!example]
>>$a_{n}=n!$ is a sequence
>>$a_{1}=1!=1$
>>$a_{2}=2!=2$
>>$a_{3}=3!=6$
>>$\dots$
